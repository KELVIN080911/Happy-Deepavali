What is deepavali ?

Diwali, also spelled Divali, is one of the major religious festivals in Hinduism, Jainism, and Sikhism. The name is derived from the Sanskrit term dipavali, meaning “row of lights.” The festival generally symbolizes the victory of light over darkness

How families and friends get together to celebrate the Indian festival of lights

  1. Spending time together. People often visit their relatives during Diwali to spend time and celebrate the holiday with their loved ones.
  2. Cooking up a feast
  3. Lighting up the holiday
